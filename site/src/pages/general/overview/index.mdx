## Introduction

Welcome to the hands-on lab page for IBM QRadar SIEM. You can find detailed step-by-step guidance on how to progress through the lab here. The whole lab takes approximately 3 hours to finish.

Each participant has his own environment hosted in IBM Cloud to play around with. Most likely you were given a participant number before the start of the workshop. Please use this number and look for your environment details under the [Environment Access](/general/environment-access) section.

The whole technical lab revolves around QRadar Now Demo available for deployment and reservation on IBM Techzone. IBMers and business partners can provision this instance here [Techzone resource page](https://techzone.ibm.com/resource/61dc81c7959c52001f1ee25f)

## Lab Technical Content

During the 3 hours you will work with IBM QRadar SIEM. You will acquire deeper technical knowledge on how the product functions and operates, what capabilities it has and how it integrates with other security software tools.
